Escape Soy Wax Candles & Gifts

Hand poured scented candles, made locally in our studio, Using 100% natural sustainable ingredients sourced within the UK.


Hello, My Name is Julie and I am the founder and creator of Escape Soy Wax Candles & Gifts. I have always loved melting candles and burning melts since I can remember.
During Covid, I was placed on furlough by my employer and took the opportunity to find out how to make these for myself. For 2 years, I researched, tried and tested many recipes which are now my bespoke formula which I use today.
I hope you enjoy looking around my website, Please feel free to get in touch.
