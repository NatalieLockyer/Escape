Escape Soy Wax Candles & Gifts

Hand poured scented candles, made locally in our studio, Using 100% natural, and sustainable, ingredients sourced within the UK.


"Escape Soy Wax Candles and Gifts" was founded and created by Julie. Having been placed on furlough during the covid pandemic, Julie made the decision to turn a hobby, and passion, into a new opportunity.


Having always had an interest in candles and wax melts, especially after a long day at work, Julie researched, and then tried and tested her own unique recipes and is now proud to use her own bespoke formula.


Feel free to 'Escape' and explore our products page and we invite any questions or queries via our contact us page.
